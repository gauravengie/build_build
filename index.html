Building Ontology

The PLATOON Building ontology module extends SEAS Building ontology () which describes a taxonomy of buildings, building spaces, and rooms.

Notice that there is no single accepted conceptualization of the building domain. It exists different categorizations according to different axes such as usage, occupancy, activity, energy efficiency, and insulations..;

This ontology may overlap with the following ontologies and should minimize and align with:

  • gbXML ontology (https://sjorsvangool.github.io/ogbxml/)
  • ifcOWL ontology (https://standards.buildingsmart.org/IFC/DEV/IFC4/ADD2_TC1/OWL/index.html)
  • BOT (https://w3c-lbd-cg.github.io/bot/)
  • Brick(https://brickschema.org/)
  • Saref for building (https://saref.etsi.org/saref4bldg/v1.1.2/)
  • And the work of Linked Building Data community group

Please download the alignement file here (il faut mettre le fichier alignement ici) 

The building taxonomy shows, on one hand, the concepts reused from other ontologies such as seas, schema, bot, and CIM; in another hand, the concepts created in the Platoon project (shown in bold) such as, IndoorZone and OutDoorZone; ClimateZone and BuildingGroup.

As noticed previously, a concept or relation is added only if it does not already exist in other existing ontology or it exists, but the meaning does not fit the need of the pilot.
